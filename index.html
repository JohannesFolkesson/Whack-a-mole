Whack-A-Mole

Game Instructions

Goal: Whack as many moles as possible before time runs out!

How to play:

  • Click Start to begin the game
  • Moles will pop up randomly on the board
  • Click quickly on the moles to score points
  • You have 60 seconds
  • Click Reset to start over

Scoring: Each hit gives you points. Try to beat your record!
